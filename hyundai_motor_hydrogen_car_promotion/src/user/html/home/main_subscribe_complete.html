Hyundai Motor Company has been operating the world‚Äôs first hydrogen-powered garbage truck since 2021. With reduced noise, zero gas emissions, and the ability to purify surrounding air, the hydrogen-powered garbage truck provides a better working environment for sanitation workers who stand up to the challenge of protecting our everyday lives.  

The only byproduct of this truck is pure water. By connecting the water flow to a portable sink attached to the vehicle, it serves clean water for hand washing while contributing to a cleaner future as well. 

Our zero-emission garbage truck‚Äôs hydrogen-to-water mechanism works in four steps. 
Step 1. Acquire oxygen by purifying the air 
Step 2. Generate electricity by converting hydrogen and oxygen 
Step 3. Emit purified air and pure H2O only  
Step 4. Connect the water flow to the portable sink for hand washing
