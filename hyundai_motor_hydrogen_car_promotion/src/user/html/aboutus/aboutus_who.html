Hydrogen for a Sustainable Future

Hyundai Motor Company is moving beyond mobility towards creating a sustainable future with a circular, hydrogen-based economy. Based on hydrogen fuel cell technologies, and integrated solutions that will be applied to entire cities, Hyundai will start at the first stage with electrification, and will then go on to create a hydrogen society with a smart city infrastructure for a truly sustainable future. The goal is to make hydrogen readily available for everyone, for everything, and everywhere. We aim to deliver practical solutions for the sustainable development of humanity, and through these breakthroughs help foster a global hydrogen-based society.
