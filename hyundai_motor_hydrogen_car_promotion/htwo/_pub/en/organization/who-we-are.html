Who we are

HTWO, technology for humanity

HTWO - Who we are

Hydrogen fuel cells
benefit humanity

Hyundai Motor Co.'s 'Progress for Humanity' motto expresses its pursuit of technological advancement for the progress of humanity.

The fuel cell system, created through more than 20 years of research and development, represents Hyundai's commitment to creating a new paradigm—the hydrogen society.

Today, Hyundai is continuing its 'Progress for Humanity' journey with HTWO by expanding fuel cell technology beyond automobiles.

Humanity meets hydrogen

The HTWO logo neatly encapsulates the 'Hydrogen for Humanity' message. Two interlocking Hs stand for Hydrogen and Humanity, and the twin Hs also create the '+' positive charge symbol used in fuel cell chemistry.

The new wave for

Hyundai Motor Group's vision for a hydrogen society is that by 2040, hydrogen energy will go far beyond transportation and affect all aspects of human life and industry.
The goal is to make hydrogen friendly and broadly usable "for everyone, everything and everywhere."
HTWO: We're the driving force behind Hyundai Motor Group's vision for a future hydrogen society.
